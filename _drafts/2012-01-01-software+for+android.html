--- layout: post title: Software for Android date: '2012-01-01T00:42:00.000-08:00' author: David Coles tags: modified_time: '2012-01-01T00:42:54.802-08:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-4144304653010663466.post-7138655205369700449 --- Here's a selection of fun/useful/interesting apps I've found for Android:

☆ - Free/Open Source Software (links to associated project)
($x.xx) - Price for paid apps


Utilities

  • ADW Launcher - Alternate Homescreen Launcher with theme support. The default for CyanogenMod.
  • Barcode Scanner - An excellent barcode/QR reader. Can also generate QR codes to share. ☆
  • Tasker ($5.99 USD)
Communication
  • BEEM - XMPP Client.  ☆
  • Yaaic - Excellent IRC client with support for multiple servers.  ☆
Games
  • ScummVM - Android port of the excellent ScummVM engine for a whole bunch of point-and-click adventure games from the 90s. Great if you have some old games laying around.  ☆
  • Twisty - z-machine interpreter for reading interactive fiction, such as Zork. ☆
