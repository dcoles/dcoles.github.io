Google's smeared leap-second NTP service is now publicly available.
Rather than having a 61 second minute, this service smears out the difference in the 10 hours before and 10 hours after by running the clock ever so slightly slower (0.0014% slow to be exact). This is the same time used in all Google APIs.

For more technical details check out https://developers.google.com/time/
