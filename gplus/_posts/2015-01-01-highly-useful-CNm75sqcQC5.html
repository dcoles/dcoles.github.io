One of my favorite Android features, that barely anyone knows:

Go into Android Drive, in a file-listing view. Hit the big (+) create button, then Scan.

Point it at, say, a receipt, and press the shutter button, then confirm, and it saves it as a pdf to your drive. You can rotate, crop, choose bw/color/etc, take multiple pages into a single pdf. Not only that, but Drive will then recognize and index all the text in the image so you can find it later.Â
