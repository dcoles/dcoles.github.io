Since it's been getting a bit of traction on social media, Republican Mike D. Rogers has introduced a bill (H.R.193 - American Sovereignty Restoration Act of 2017) which calls for the United States to terminate their membership of the United Nations and closure of U.S. Missions to the United Nations.

Time to panic?

Well, the same bill was introduced back in at the start of the last two sessions of congress [EDIT: Actually, every congress for the last 20 years!] (H.R.1205 - American Sovereignty Restoration Act of 2015 and H.R.75 - American Sovereignty Restoration Act of 2013) and both went absolutely nowhere. The one and only action was to be referred to House Foreign Affairs committee - the same for what has happened to this bill.
